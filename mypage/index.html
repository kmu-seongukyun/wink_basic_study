Made in Canada

Gridbooks are designed by Sajak & Farki and printed by Kallen Printing. These handmade notebooks are the best way to visualize and sketch digital ideas. They combine familiar dot grids with technology asspect ratios and column indicators. If you're going to reach for a piece of paper, make sure it's in a Gridbook.
